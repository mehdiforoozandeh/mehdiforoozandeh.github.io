About

I am a a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Simon Fraser University, where I focus on advancing the field of bioinformatics through machine learning approaches. My research centers on genome annotation, with particular emphasis on developing computational methods to understand and predict biological functions.

Currently, I work at Roche, where I apply my expertise in computational biology to real-world problems in pharmaceutical research and development. My work bridges the gap between academic research and practical applications in healthcare.
